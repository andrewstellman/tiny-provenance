DETECTIVE: I told you this case would not go away.
SUSPECT: You say that about every case.
DETECTIVE: Yes, because they never do.
SUSPECT: Then why are you here, detective?
DETECTIVE: Because you still have not told me where you were on Tuesday.
SUSPECT: I told you, I was at the library.
DETECTIVE: The library was closed on Tuesday.
SUSPECT: Then I was at the cafe.
DETECTIVE: Which cafe?
SUSPECT: The one across from the library.
DETECTIVE: That cafe was also closed.
SUSPECT: Detective, you are very difficult.
DETECTIVE: And you, my friend, are very forgetful.
SUSPECT: Perhaps I was at home.
DETECTIVE: Perhaps you were.
SUSPECT: Then we are done.
DETECTIVE: We are not done. I told you this case would not go away.
